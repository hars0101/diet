Aim

  • The Drone Research and Development Lab in collaboration with Dronacharya Tech-Hub Provides Drone Project based learning solu- tions tailored for technical education.
  • The Drone Research and Development lab offer a hand on inter disciplinary approach, empowering students to apply the theoretical knowledge in real world scenarios using drone technology.

Objectives

  • The main objective of Drone Research and Development lab is to make the students under- stand the latest technology of drone's that will be helpful in solving the real-world problems, using artificial intelligence, machine learning and deep learning algorithms, that will be used with the companion computers on the Drone.

Goals

  • Educating Students about the DRONE.
  • Participating in Hackathons, Competitions and Tech events.
  • Promote Professional Exploration And Networking.

Collaborations

Workshops Conducted

Space Chase Pvt X Diet

Conducted in collaboration with Space Chase Pvt.,Participants engaged in hands-on sessions to build and integrate drones while learning flight control techniques. The workshop provided practical experience and insights into drone assembly and operation.

Dronacharya Tech Hub X Diet

Conducted in collaboration with Dronacharya Tech Hub , Participants learned drone basics, components, assembly, maintenance, calibration, and precision flying. Advanced sessions covered sensors, system integration, and industry roles. Teams gained hands-on experience and prepared for real-world applications, including internships.
